<Para>🎨 Design Insight</Para>

White space, often misunderstood as empty, plays a crucial role in design.

It helps guide user attention, improves readability, and creates a sense of balance.

<br/>

Good design is not about adding more — it's about removing unnecessary elements and letting content breathe.
