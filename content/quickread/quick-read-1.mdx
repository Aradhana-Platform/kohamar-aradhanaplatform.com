<Para>📡 Featured Article</Para>

Ambient computing is changing the way humans interact with machines — without screens, clicks, or even words.

Devices are becoming smarter, more context-aware, and capable of understanding human intent without explicit input.

<br/>

Instead of traditional interfaces, technology is blending seamlessly into our environment. Voice assistants, smart homes, and AI-driven systems are early examples of this shift.

<br/>

This silent revolution is not just about convenience — it's about redefining human-computer interaction itself.
